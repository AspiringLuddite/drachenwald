The Kingdom of Drachenwald is a part of the Society for Creative Anachronism, Inc. (SCA), a not-for-profit educational organisation which studies the Middle Ages by recreating the pastimes and crafts of the period. International in scope, our activities include tournaments, festivals, classes, feasts, and all manner of arts and sciences you would find in the culture of pre-1600s western Europe. We actively welcome new members!
